Privacy Policy

At cryptodigitalpro, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information with transparency and responsibility.

Information We Collect How We Use Your Data Cookies & Analytics Data Protection Confidentiality Rights Updates

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following:

  • Basic contact data (name, email, phone).
  • Case details submitted for recovery or dispute assistance.
  • Technical data such as browser type, IP address, and device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to process requests, verify identity, provide service updates, and ensure account security. We never sell or share your personal information with unauthorized third parties.

3. Cookies & Analytics

Cookies help us enhance your user experience, remember preferences, and measure site performance. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

4. Data Protection

All data is encrypted and stored on secure servers. Only authorized staff members have access to your case information, following strict internal policies.

5. Confidentiality Rights

Clients can request to view, correct, or delete their data at any time. Every case is handled under confidentiality agreements ensuring full privacy compliance.

6. Updates

Our Privacy Policy may evolve with time to reflect legal, security, or service improvements. Updates will always be published here with the revision date.
